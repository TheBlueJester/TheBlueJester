Welcome to World of Toys!


World of Toys is a family owned and operated toy store in the heart of Sydney.
We have a passion for the joy that toys, gadgets and games can bring into the lives of loved ones. We strive to have a wide range of products that have been played and approved by our team to ensure the quality and fun factor of all our products.



In order to help you find what you're looking for as quickly and easily as possible we've included a store map right here on our site. Please take a look at our wonderful selection, enjoy yourself, and we hope to see you in store soon!





If we don't have something you're looking for and you don't know where else to go we are happy to help you source a product from one the supplies in our Network. If we can't find it for you then it probably doesn't exist!
